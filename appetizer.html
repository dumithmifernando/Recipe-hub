Appetizer Recipes

Bruschetta

Ingredients:

  • Baguette slices
  • Tomatoes
  • Fresh basil
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt & pepper

Steps:

  1. Toast baguette slices.
  2. Mix chopped tomatoes, basil, olive oil, garlic, salt & pepper.
  3. Spoon mixture onto toasted bread.

Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients:

  • Button mushrooms
  • Cream cheese
  • Garlic
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Parsley
  • Bread crumbs

Steps:

  1. Remove mushroom stems and chop them finely.
  2. Mix with cream cheese, garlic, parmesan, and parsley.
  3. Stuff mushrooms and sprinkle with bread crumbs.
  4. Bake until golden.
