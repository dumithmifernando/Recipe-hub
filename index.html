1. Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp milk (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp butter or oil

Method:

Crack the eggs into a bowl and add the milk if you're using it, along with a pinch of salt and pepper. Whisk everything together until the mixture is smooth and a little frothy. Heat the butter or oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat. Once hot, pour in the eggs and gently stir them with a spatula. Continue stirring until the eggs are softly set but still moist. Remove from the heat and serve immediately while warm.

2. Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese (any kind)
  • 1 tbsp butter

Method:

Begin by spreading butter on one side of each slice of bread. Place one slice, buttered side down, onto a skillet or pan, and lay the cheese slices on top. Cover with the second slice of bread, buttered side facing up. Cook the sandwich over medium heat until the bottom is golden brown, then flip it and cook the other side. Once both sides are crispy and the cheese is melted, remove from the pan, slice, and enjoy hot.

3. Banana Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup milk (or almond milk)
  • 1 tsp honey (optional)
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon (optional)
  • 3–4 ice cubes

Method:

To make this quick and healthy smoothie, place the banana, milk, honey (if you're using it), cinnamon, and ice cubes into a blender. Blend on high until the mixture is smooth and creamy. Once fully blended, pour the smoothie into a glass and serve chilled. It makes a refreshing drink for breakfast or a light snack.

4. Pasta with Garlic & Olive Oil (Aglio e Olio)

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti or any pasta
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic (sliced)
  • Salt to taste
  • Crushed red chili flakes (optional)
  • Fresh parsley (optional)

Method:

Cook the pasta according to the instructions on the package until it's al dente, then drain it and set aside some of the pasta water. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat and add the sliced garlic. Sauté the garlic until it turns golden and fragrant, being careful not to burn it. Add chili flakes if you like some heat. Toss in the cooked pasta along with a few spoonfuls of the reserved pasta water. Stir everything together until the pasta is evenly coated. Add salt and sprinkle with chopped parsley if desired, then serve warm.
