Dessert Recipes

Chocolate Brownies

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Steps:

  1. Melt butter and mix with sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  2. Add cocoa, flour, and salt.
  3. Pour into greased pan and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 20–25 minutes.

Strawberry Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter (melted)
  • 16 oz cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • Fresh strawberries (for topping)

Steps:

  1. Mix crumbs, sugar, and butter for crust. Press into pan.
  2. Beat cream cheese, sugar, eggs, and vanilla. Pour over crust.
  3. Bake at 325°F (160°C) for 45–50 mins. Cool and top with strawberries.
